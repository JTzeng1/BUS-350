What This Website Covers

The CSUMB Financial Hub was created to help students understand college costs and make smart financial decisions. Paying for college can be overwhelming, but this site breaks everything down in a simple and easy-to-understand way.

  • Budgeting: Learn how to track your spending and create a realistic monthly budget.
  • Financial Planning: Understand tuition, cost of attendance, grants, scholarships, and loans.
  • Scholarships: Discover free money opportunities that help reduce student debt.
  • Student Employment: Learn about on-campus jobs, OtterJobs, and Federal Work-Study.
  • Investing: Get beginner-friendly information about saving and investing for the future.

Smart Ways to Save Money at CSUMB

College is expensive, but there are many ways CSUMB students can lower their costs and avoid unnecessary debt. Here are some practical money-saving tips:

  • Apply for scholarships every year — even small awards add up.
  • Fill out the FAFSA early to maximize grants and financial aid.
  • Live with parents or share housing to reduce rent costs.
  • Use campus resources like the library instead of buying textbooks.
  • Work part-time or join Federal Work-Study to earn money while in school.
  • Track your spending and avoid unnecessary subscriptions and eating out.
