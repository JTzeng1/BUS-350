Finding Work While in College

OtterJobs – CSUMB Career Portal

OtterJobs is the official online career portal for California State University, Monterey Bay (CSUMB). It connects students and alumni with internships, on-campus jobs, part-time and full-time roles, as well as summer positions. The platform is powered by Handshake, making it easy to search, apply, and manage your opportunities.

For students, OtterJobs is a free resource to explore available positions that can help you earn money while pursuing your studies. Employers can post internships or part/full-time roles targeting CSUMB students. Whether you're looking to gain experience, earn extra income, or build your resume, OtterJobs is the go-to resource.

You can access OtterJobs directly through the CSUMB portal here: CSUMB OtterJobs.

Other OtterJobs References

Be aware that the term "OtterJobs" can also refer to a UK-based recruitment agency in Hampshire providing tailored staffing solutions, or to Otter.ai, an AI company offering careers in engineering and software roles. However, for CSUMB students, the career board is the most relevant resource for finding work during college.

Working on Campus

CSUMB Student Employment

Working on campus is a great way to earn income, gain hands-on experience, and become part of the CSUMB community. Students can find positions that fit their schedules while building skills that complement their studies. Positions include Student Assistants, Instructional Student Assistants, Graduate Assistants, and Teaching Associates.

All available student employment opportunities, hiring procedures, and necessary forms are listed on the official CSUMB Student Employment page. Students can also find guidance for international students and details about work-study positions.

Federal Work-Study

Earn Money and Gain Experience

Federal Work-Study is a government-funded program that allows students with financial need to work up to 20 hours per week on campus or with an approved local organization. If Work-Study is part of your financial aid package, you can join hundreds of students working across offices, academic departments, and campus eateries at Cal State Monterey Bay.

Even if you are not part of the Federal Work-Study Program, you can still explore other student employment opportunities on campus to earn income and gain professional experience.
